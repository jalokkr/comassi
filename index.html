join us and reap the benefits

regenagri is an international regenerative agriculture program for securing the health of the land and the wealth of those who live on it. It supports farms and organisations transitioning to holistic farming, increasing soil health, encouraging biodiversity, reducing green house gas emissions and sequestering CO2. regenagri also provides farmers with routes to additional funds through carbon credit markets and environmental subsidies.
In supporting regenerative farming, we aid in the regeneration of our planet and sustainably secure the future of agricultural products and their supply chains.
